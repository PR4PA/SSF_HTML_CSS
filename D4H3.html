What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is the customer company. We make cloud-based software designed to help
businesses connect to their customers in a whole new way, so they can find more
prospects, close more deals, and wow customers with amazing service.

Customer 360, our complete suite of products, unites your sales, service, marketing,
commerce, and IT teams with a single, shared view of customer information, so that your
company can become a customer company, too.
